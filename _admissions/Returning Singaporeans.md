There is a centralised exercise conducted by MOE for Returning Singapore Citizens and new Permanent Residents. Further information is available at&nbsp;[https://www.clementitownsec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/returning-singaporeans/](https://www.clementitownsec.moe.edu.sg/admissions/returning-singaporeans/).  

Information on the School Placement Exercise for Returning Singaporeans is available at&nbsp; [https://www.seab.gov.sg/spers-sec/](https://www.seab.gov.sg/spers-sec/)
