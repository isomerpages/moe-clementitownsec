There is a centralised exercise conducted by MOE for Returning Singapore Citizens and new Permanent Residents. Further information is available at&nbsp;[https://www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/returning-singaporeans/](https://www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/returning-singaporeans/).  

Information on the School Placement Exercise for Returning Singaporeans is available at&nbsp;[https://www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/spers-sec](https://www.moe.gov.sg/education/admissions/spers-sec)/
