International students who wish to seek admission to MOE mainstream schools at the Secondary 1 to 3 levels must take part in the&nbsp;[https://www.moe.gov.sg/international-students/aeis](https://www.moe.gov.sg/international-students/aeis).  
  

The school is unable to accept direct applications from international students.

More information can be found at:&nbsp;         [https://www.moe.gov.sg/international-students](https://www.moe.gov.sg/international-students)
