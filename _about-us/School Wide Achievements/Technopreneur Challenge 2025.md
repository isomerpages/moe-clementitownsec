<p>Under the CT Humanities Talent Development Programme, 12 Secondary 3 Geography
Clementeens represented Clementi Town Sec at the Technopreneur Challenge
2025, co-organised by JA International, HP, and Southwest CDC.</p>
<p>Three teams pitched bold, sustainability-focused ideas to industry leaders
at HP HQ. Team TOCR impressed the judges with their innovative use of moss-based
biotechnology to boost solar panel efficiency in low-light conditions,
clinching 2nd Runner-Up.</p>
<p>Congratulations to Jensen, Michelle, Kelly, and Yoga from 316!</p>
<p>Special mention to:</p>
<p>Team Unmapped Waymark (Charlotte, Pollin, Chenxi &amp; Hassini from 316)
for their empathetic pitch on low-cost water filtration for underprivileged
communities.</p>
<p>Team ImpaCT (Cadence, Gwen, Zen &amp; Matthew from 314) for their innovative
use of bacteria colonies to tackle ocean pollution.</p>
